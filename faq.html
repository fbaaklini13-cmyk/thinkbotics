FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions. If you don’t see what you need, contact us.

What do you sell exactly?

Done‑for‑you business automations: lead capture, follow‑ups, scheduling, support triage, notifications, and reports—wired to WhatsApp/Instagram/Gmail/Calendly and your tools.

How do you price?

Flat setup + monthly service. Tiers depend on number of automations and channels. Clear scope, cancel anytime.

Is there ongoing support?

Yes. Weekly reviews, bug fixes, and optimization are included under our SLA.

How fast can I go live?

Discovery in 48h, typical builds in 5–7 days, then launch + iterate.

Can you integrate with my CRM?

We integrate most modern CRMs via APIs or zaps. If it’s not supported, we propose a lightweight data bridge.

How secure is my data?

Principle of least privilege, encrypted transit/storage where applicable, audit logs, and role‑based access.

Which industries are best fit?

Clinics, gyms, professional services, real estate, e‑commerce, education, and hospitality.

Do you offer trials?

We prefer pilot scopes with real KPIs instead of sandbox trials to prove value quickly.
